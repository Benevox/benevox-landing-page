# Benevox (BVX) — The Software Production Machine

Benevox is a **high-discipline software delivery system** designed to connect businesses with elite tech talent across Africa. We function as a **structured production machine** rather than a traditional agency, providing a "plug-in" model for experienced dev teams and reliable delivery pipelines.

## 1. The Core Operating Principles
The entire Benevox machine is governed by **five non-negotiable rules** to ensure commercial safety and delivery integrity:
1.  **No idea goes to engineering unvalidated:** Technical feasibility must be verified before commitment.
2.  **No promise goes to the client unverified:** Sales never promises price or timeline until the technical pod validates the scope.
3.  **No milestone is invented during execution:** All milestones are designed during scoping and validated before proposal.
4.  **AI drafts, humans approve:** AI accelerates every stage (intake, scoping, risk), but humans remain the final approval authority.
5.  **Team payout stays inside paid milestone scope:** The pod only executes and is paid for work that the client has funded.

## 2. Engineering-as-a-Service (EaaS) Models
We offer three distinct engagement models tailored to business maturity and uncertainty:

*   **Model 1: Fully Scoped (Fixed-Firm):** Best for projects with **absolute requirements** (e.g., legacy migrations). Requirements are frozen upfront to prevent scope creep.
*   **Model 2: Per-Sprint (Agile-Flow):** Our default for high-speed environments. Work is executed in **one-week cycles** with a pay-as-you-go structure.
*   **Model 3: Hybrid (80/20):** Strategic visibility through a **directional roadmap** combined with a **committed sprint-validated execution**.

## 3. The AI Operations Layer
AI is a formal operating layer at Benevox, integrated into the pipeline to reduce manual waste and improve consistency:
*   **AI Intake:** Converts messy client requirements into structured summaries.
*   **AI Scoping:** Drafts task-linked milestone structures and delivery sequencing.
*   **AI Risk Agent:** Identifies dependencies, assumptions, and blockers.
*   **AI Change Control:** Analyzes the impact of "small updates" on cost and timeline.

## 4. Team Structure: The Delivery Pod
Execution is handled by cross-functional **Delivery Pods**. A standard unit consists of:
*   **Pod Product Manager (PM):** Coordinates validation and maintains progress integrity (30% share).
*   **Designer:** Executes UI/UX deliverables (10% share).
*   **Frontend Engineer:** Scoped frontend implementation (20% share).
*   **Backend Engineer:** Scoped API and system logic (25% share).
*   **QA Engineer:** Owns quality confirmation and the final 5% holdback release (15% share).

## 5. Incentive & Payout Model
Benevox uses a **progress-based payout model** rather than time-based billing:
*   **The Pod Pool:** 50% of total project revenue is allocated to the delivery pod.
*   **Quality Holdback (95/5 Rule):** 95% of validated progress is payable upon completion; the **remaining 5% is held back** until QA confirms excellence.

## 6. Technical & Design Standards
*   **Atomic Design:** All interfaces follow the Atoms → Molecules → Organisms methodology for scalability.
*   **4-Point Spacing System:** Strict adherence to 4-point increments for visual harmony.
*   **Web Accessibility:** Alignment with **WCAG 2.1 requirements**, focusing on contrast, identifiable feedback, and clear navigation.
